Hello, I am Sean Negus

Graduate Student at Iowa State University

I am currently a graduate student pursuing a master's degree in Agricultural Economics at Iowa State University. With a interest in the intersection of agriculture and economics, I am wish to contribute meaningfully to the evolving landscape of agribusiness.

Growing up on a farm I have always had a passion for agriculture which led me majoring in Agricultural Business as an undergrad. During undergrad, I also undertook a data science minor where I developed an appreciation for more analytical methods and gained experience in R and Python. Within my master's program I plan on gaining a deeper understanding of economic theory and combine it with self-learning of various programming languages and tools.

In my free time I am a big Cyclone fan and enjoy watching all the football and basketball games. I also enjoy spending my free time outdoors, cooking, reading books, and doing puzzles.

Work Experience

Growing up on a farm I was taught a wide range of practical and life skills. It teaches responsibility, as things on a farm require constant attention from the livestock to the equipment. Problem-solving skills too as things are constantly changing, such as having to adapt to weather, seasons, and other unforeseen challenges.
